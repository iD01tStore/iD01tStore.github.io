Security, Speed, and Reliability

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. The connection is established over an end-to-end encrypted ngrok tunnel. Technicians only have visual control (screen sharing via scrcpy) and a small, audited set of ADB commands necessary for the repair. Your personal files and cloud accounts are not accessed or transferred.

We follow a "no fix, no fee"-style approach. If the technician cannot resolve the issue after a genuine repair attempt, our team reviews the session and may issue a partial or full refund according to our Refund Policy. The AI diagnostic provides an estimated success probability upfront.

We specialize in software-level issues: screen lock troubleshooting (pattern/PIN), Factory Reset Protection (FRP), Mobile Device Management (MDM) issues, boot loop fixes, custom ROM problems, and severe software corruption. Hardware damage is not supported.

Download our Windows USB Client, enable USB Debugging on your device, and connect via USB cable. The client will automatically detect your device and run the AI diagnostic. macOS and Linux clients are in development.
