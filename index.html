Frequently Asked Questions

How does remote repair work?
Download our secure client, connect your Android device via USB, and log in to your account. Our technicians will remotely access your device through an encrypted connection and perform the necessary repairs. You maintain full control and can disconnect at any time.
Is my data safe during repair?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and our technicians only access system files necessary for repair. Your personal data, photos, and files remain private and untouched. All connections are GDPR compliant and audited regularly.
What if the repair doesn't work?
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If we're unable to fix your device, you won't be charged. Standard repairs include a 30-day warranty, and complex repairs include a 60-day warranty. If the issue returns within the warranty period, we'll fix it free of charge.
How long does a typical repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 15-30 minutes. Complex issues may take up to 2 hours. Our AI diagnostic runs in under 60 seconds, giving you an accurate time estimate before you commit to the repair.
Which Android devices are supported?
We support all major Android manufacturers including Samsung, Google Pixel, OnePlus, Xiaomi, OPPO, Motorola, and more. Devices running Android 8.0 or higher are fully compatible. Our AI diagnostic will confirm compatibility before starting any repair.
